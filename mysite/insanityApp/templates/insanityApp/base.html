Mission

This BCM RegistryÂ® is an online Patient Registry dedicated to a rare genetic retinal disease, the Blue Cone Monochromacy. The Registry has been created by BCM Families Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit patients' organization with the mission to eradicate Blue Cone Monochromacy.

Purpose

The Purpose of the Registry is to longitudinally collect data internationally from patients with Blue Cone Monochromacy. The Aim of this Registry is to describe the clinical features, variations in phenotype and the natural history this disease, meaning its characteristics, management, and long term outcomes with and/or without treatment.
